ChatGPT Conversation Directory

A Chrome extension that automatically generates a structured, navigable outline for long ChatGPT conversations — helping you quickly jump to any part of the discussion.

What It Does

ChatGPT conversations can quickly become long and difficult to navigate. This extension analyzes the conversation structure in real time and builds a clickable directory, allowing you to move between questions, answers, and topics with ease.

Key Features

  • Automatically generates a conversation outline
  • Clickable directory for fast navigation
  • Designed for long and complex ChatGPT sessions
  • Runs entirely locally in your browser
  • No account, no login, no cloud sync

Privacy-First by Design

All processing happens locally within your browser. No conversation data is collected, stored, or transmitted to any external server.
