Privacy Policy

For the browser extension ChatGPT Conversation Directory

Last updated: January 2026

1. Overview

ChatGPT Conversation Directory is a local-first browser extension developed by Wisteria Software Ltd. We take user privacy seriously and designed this product to operate without servers, accounts, or cloud services.

2. Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or user-identifiable information.

Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Account credentials or authentication data
  • Device identifiers or tracking IDs
  • Browsing history or behavioral analytics
  • ChatGPT conversation content outside your local browser

3. Local Processing

All functionality of the extension is performed entirely within your browser.

Conversation content is analyzed locally only for the purpose of generating a structured, navigable directory. No conversation data is sent to any external server.

4. Servers and Accounts

This product operates with:

  • No backend servers
  • No user accounts or login system
  • No cloud synchronization
  • No remote data storage

5. Third-Party Services

The extension does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, tracking libraries, or external APIs.

6. Permissions

Any browser permissions requested by the extension are used solely to provide its core functionality and are not used for data collection, tracking, or profiling.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the revised version will be published on this page with an updated revision date.
