Privacy Policy

For the browser extension ChatGPT Conversation Directory

Last updated: January 2026

1. Overview

ChatGPT Conversation Directory is a local-first browser extension developed by Wisteria Software Ltd. It operates without servers, accounts, or cloud services.

2. Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or user-identifiable information.

3. Local Processing

All functionality is performed entirely within your browser. Conversation content is processed locally and never sent to any server.

4. Third-Party Services

No third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services are used.
